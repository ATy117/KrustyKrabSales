Customers

      Salmon

      The most common customers in the Krusty Krab. Salmon makes up 60% of the total population in Bikini Bottom.

      Seahorse

      Seahorses are the equivalent of actual horses on the surface. The most notable is Mystery.

      Sea Lion

      The sea lions are known for their tan skin and incrdeible body built here in Bikini Bottom. The most famous of them all is none other than Craig Mammalton. He is known to be the apple of everyone's eyes.

      Leatherback Turtle

      The turtles are known to be the travelers of Bikini Bottom. They may be rare, but they're the most loyal customers of the Krusty Krab.

      Giant Clam

      A Giant Clam can take the place of animals such as wolves, as they are sometimes shown shown howling ("Texas") and attacking people ("Tea at the Treedome"). The most notable clam is Old Bluelip.

      Gray Whale

      The Gray Whales are known for their extraordinary built and huge size. They are frequently seen along Mussel Beach together with Larry the Lobster.

      Coral

      Corals are considered as pets here in Bikini Bottom. Their owners usually buy them Krabby Patties as a special treat for when they're in their best behaviour.
